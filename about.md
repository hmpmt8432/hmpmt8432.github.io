# About Me

I am a project-minded professional with a background in leadership, operations, and continuous improvement, and I am actively expanding my capabilities in cybersecurity. My goal is to combine structured program and project management practices with technical security knowledge to solve problems, improve processes, and support resilient systems.

This portfolio is designed to show not only what I have studied, but what I have built, documented, and learned through practical work. I believe strong documentation, measurable outcomes, and repeatable processes are just as important as technical implementation.

My current effort is focused on building a cybersecurity homelab that allows me to simulate enterprise-style environments and document projects involving identity management, firewall configuration, system hardening, logging, security monitoring, vulnerability management, and incident response.

I am especially interested in roles and projects at the intersection of cybersecurity, governance, operations, and program execution.

## What This Portfolio Shows

This site is intended to document:
